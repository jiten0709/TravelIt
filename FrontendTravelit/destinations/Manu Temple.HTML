MANU TEMPLE

Manu Temple is an old temple located at a distance of 3 km from the main market of Manali. The entire shrine of Manu Temple is dedicated to the Manu – the Indian Sage, who was acknowledged to be the creator of this world. In fact, some legends rely heavily on the fact that the present-day temple is the exact place where sage Manu would meditate regularly. Unlike other temples that stood with it, this temple was never built for the ones who carry their hearts on their sleeves. To make your way to the shrine, you need to trespass a narrow road, which in itself seems to be nothing less than a thrilling adventure. Yet, the adventure buffs will find this place to be a perfect blend of comfort and exploration.
